What is lore in gaming?

What is Lore in Gaming, retrived from https://medium.com/illumination/the-meaning-and-value-of-lore-in-gaming-6166a6637b6c

Lore is a term that refers to the body of knowledge, stories, and traditions that are passed down from one generation to another. It is a type of folklore that is associated with mythology, legend, and fantasy.

Lore can take many forms, including myths, legends, fables, fairy tales, and folklore. It can be found in many different cultures and is often used to explain the origins of things, such as the world or the human race, and to provide moral lessons and guidance.

One of the most well-known examples of lore is Norse mythology, which includes stories about gods and goddesses such as Odin, Thor, and Loki.

These traditional stories have been passed down through generations and have become a part of popular culture.

Lore can also be found in video games, particularly fantasy and science fiction. Lore provides backstory and world-building in many games, creating a rich and immersive gaming experience.

In addition to providing entertainment, lore can also be used to teach important lessons and values.

Many fables, for example, are designed to teach children about the consequences of their actions and the importance of making good choices. Similarly, the mythological stories of ancient civilizations often taught lessons about morality and ethics.

Generally, lore is a rich and complex body of knowledge encompassing many different forms and functions.

Whether used for entertainment, education, or cultural preservation, lore shapes our understanding of the virtual world.

《:山海经》中有言：“沧海之中，有度朔之山，上有大桃木，其屈蟠三千里，其枝间东北曰鬼门，万鬼所出入也。” 度朔源于古代传说，乃遥远东海一处山名，在多部古籍中均有提及。除作“度朔山”外，度朔还常用于对神秘的山岳或圣地的象征与描述。

成书于战国时期至汉代初期，所著乃远古文化，记录着大荒时期先祖们的行迹与神思，勾勒出遥远的彼时之文明与文化形态。 譬如山海经中曾记载有一远古部族，为葛天氏。相传有葛天氏之乐，由三人操牛尾而歌唱，共八曲，乃“乐舞”之发始，是为乐舞始祖。 其中所著包罗万千，又以所记载的奇异生物广为人知。例如:灭蒙鸟便是其间所描述的一种。
